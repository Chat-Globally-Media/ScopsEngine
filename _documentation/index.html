Installation
Step 1

Create a new database.

Step 2

Create 3 subdomains: m.{yourdomain.com} , chat.{yourdomain.com} and admin.{yourdomain.com}. Where {yourdomain.com} is your Domain name.

Step 3

Upload files from Script to your domain and sudomains.

  • web.scops -> {domain.com}
  • m.scops -> m.{domain.com}
  • admin.scops -> admin.{domain.com}
  • chat.scops -> chat.{domain.com}

Where {yourdomain.com} is your Domain name.

Step 4

Run and fill up these forms.

  • {domain.com}/install.php
  • m.{domain.com}/install.php
  • admin.{domain.com}/install.php
  • chat.{domain.com}/install.php

Where {yourdomain.com} is your Domain name.

Step 5

Your social network is ready.
